Our Story

A Revolution in the Heart of Mithila

MIC of Mithila was born from a simple idea — to give a stage to the unheard voices of Mithila. It started as a small gathering in Madhubani and soon transformed into a vibrant platform for youth, artists, and creators to express themselves freely.

By organizing dozens of successful open mic events, we've created not just performances but a cultural movement — reintroducing the spirit of art in Mithila and nurturing local talent.

MIC of Mithila isn't just a stage — it's a spotlight. We provide professional setups, photography, wide social media reach, and an encouraging crowd.
